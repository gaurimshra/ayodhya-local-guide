Ramayan & Ancient Roots

Ayodhya is revered as the birthplace of Lord Ram and the heart of the Ramayan. The epic has shaped the city’s moral values, traditions, and way of life for thousands of years.

Traditions & Festivals

Diwali – The Festival of Lights

Diwali in Ayodhya marks the return of Lord Ram. Thousands of diyas illuminate the ghats, creating a divine and unforgettable sight.

Ghat Culture & Spiritual Life

The Sarayu ghats are central to daily spiritual life. Morning rituals, evening aartis, and prayers connect devotees deeply with faith and nature.

Art, Architecture & Landmarks

Ayodhya’s temples reflect traditional Hindu architecture, intricate carvings, and symbolic design passed through generations.

Local Life & Cuisine

Life in Ayodhya is simple and peaceful. The cuisine reflects devotion and tradition, offering vegetarian dishes like kachori, poori sabzi, and kesar peda.
